# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in Elemental Orbit respectful, welcoming, and free from harassment for everyone, regardless of experience level, identity, background, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and specific.
- Welcome questions and differing levels of scientific or technical experience.
- Critique ideas and evidence rather than people.
- Cite authoritative sources when correcting scientific content.
- Respect privacy and do not publish another person’s private information.
- Accept maintainer decisions about project scope and moderation.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, and repeated bad-faith engagement are not tolerated in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this policy. Reports will be handled as privately and fairly as practical.

To report conduct concerns, email `krakenunbound@gmail.com`. For security vulnerabilities, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead of using a public issue.

## Scope

This policy applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other spaces where someone is representing the project.
